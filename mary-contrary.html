Persona 2

Name: Mary Contrary

Tech:

  • Laptop Computer: HP AMD Ryzen3 running Windows 11 using Chrome as browser.
  • Smart Phone: iPhone12 Mini running IOS 15.2.1 using Safari as browser.

Proficiency:

  • HP casual user, loves the touch screen aspect of the Laptop for easy usage.Only uses smartphone when on the go.

Attitudes & Needs:

  • Mary is an organic consumer and is always seeking local sourced products so she can incorporate them into her and her family's diet.
  • Mary is always happy when finding up-to-date new consistent producers and organic products.
  • Mary doesn't like it when she can't browse specific items she is looking for or find consistent producers.
  • Mary likes the Garden Connect site because it allows her to connect with local producers and search for her specified need/wants... unlike others.
  • Role: Casual user

User Story:

As a casual user I want to find locally sourced crops.

Use-Case & Interaction Flow

Pre-Condition: Mary Contrary is looking through her fridge when she realizes she is running low on lettuce. She then hops on the Garden Connect website and clicks into the search bar, enters "lettuce" and presses search. She sorts the results by closest to furthest, finds someone close with lettuce and messages them.

  • Mary clicks into the search bar.
  • The search bar indicator flashes letting Mary know she can now input whatever she'd like.
  • Mary types in "lettuce" and presses search.
  • Website loads accordingly to her search and displays the results from top to bottom.
  • Mary clicks the "sort by" drop down menu to filter her search results.
  • The drop menu displays all the sort options.
  • Mary clicks the nearest to furthest option.
  • The results page refreshes with nearest at top and furthest at bottom.
  • Mary finds some lettuce she likes then clicks "message" to communicate with the owner of the lettuce.
  • The website opens the messenger indicating that Mary can now message the owner of the lettuce.
  • Mary tells the owner when she is available to pick up some lettuce, presses send and waits for a response.
  • The messenger posts Marys message into the chat for both the owner and Mary to see.
  • Mary clicks out of the messenger.
  • The messenger drops and now displays the search results page she was previously at.
