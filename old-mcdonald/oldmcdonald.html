Name:Old McDonald

Technology

    Devices

  • McDonald has an up-to-date MacBook from 2018.
  • McDonald uses a Google phone, the Pixel 4 edition

    • Proficiency

  • Mcdonald is proficient and competent with the technology he uses. He has used a number of other websites in the past to aid him in the success of his farm, ie. the Work Away site and the WWOF site. He uses his phone regularly to coordinate with volunteers and to sell or trade his produce.

    • Feelings on Technology

  • McDonald enjoys the technology that he uses because it makes his life easier regarding the farm. He also enjoys being able to look up information and solutions re: gardening. He is excited about the Garden Connect website because it will allow him to centralize his communication regarding the farm. He only spends the amount of time necessary using technology, it is not something he uses for entertainment.

    • Needs and Wants

  • McDonald wants a convenient way to seek help for, or offer produce from his farm.
  • He is interested in finding out more about other gardens and homesteads around him.
  • He also is seeking more community and people with common interests
  • He is always making phone calls, sending e-mails, and text messages, which can get confusing, so he wants a centralized place to communicate with people and coordinate times, trades, etc.
  • He is not interested in social media and does not want to engage in a platform that is a popularity contest.

    • User Story

  • As a competent but casual user, I want to connect with people in my area about farming, gardening, livestock, etc. and be able to both seek and offer help, trades, and products.

    • Use-Case and Interaction Flow

  • Pre-condition: McDonald is logged into his Garden Connect account and on the home page. Post-condition: McDonald has made a post seeking help for fixing a t-post fence, including a picture of the broken fence.
  1. McD is already logged in, clicks on the "Make a New Post" nav item at the top of the site.
  2. This navigates McD to a page with two categories, there is text prompting him to choose either "seeking" or "offering".
  3. McD clicks on "seeking".
  4. A new page then opens up within the "seeking" category
  5. On this page is an input form with a spot for a post title, text input area, and option to link a photo.
  6. McD writes his title, then his body of the post where he describes the task that he needs help with, and then clicks on the "add photo" option.
  7. A finder window opens with the option to get a photo from his computer.
  8. McD opens up his pictures folder and finds the picture of his broken fence.
  9. McD chooses the picture and then clicks "Open".
  10. The picture is uploaded and sits beneath the text body of the post
  11. McD also wants to leave a review for another user from Garden Connect who helped him last week.
  12. To find this user, he clicks on his own username/home link in the navbar.
  13. This takes him to a sparse self-profile page where a link for his message history is located.
  14. He clicks on message history.
  15. A side bar window opens with his message history in it.
  16. He scrolls through to find the thread with the woman who helped him last week.
  17. He clicks on her username.
  18. Her profile page opens up with just her username and her about me blurb. Under this is a "leave a review" button.
  19. He clicks "leave a review"
  20. A new page opens up with a text input area, and the prompt to "leave a review".
  21. McD types a raving review of Sally and her work ethic. Clicks "leave review".
  22. This posts the review and directs him back to Sally's profile page.
  23. McD then clicks log out from a dropdown menu up in the navbar.
  24. McD is now logged out of Garden Connect.
