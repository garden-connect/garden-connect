ERD Diagram

Conceptual Model

    Profile

    • profileId (primary key)
    • profileAbout
    • profileActivationToken (for account verification)
    • profileEmail
    • profileHash (for account password)
    • profileName

    Posts

    • postId (primary key)
    • postProfileId (foreign key)
    • postActive
    • postCategory
    • postContent
    • postDate
    • postPicture
    • postTitle

    Conversation

    • conversationId (primary key)
    • conversationPostID (foreign key)
    • conversationReceiveProfileId (foreign key)
    • conversationSendProfileId (foreign key)
    • conversationContent
    • conversationDate

    Rating/Review

    • ratingReviewedProfileId (foreign key)
    • ratingReviewingProfileId (foreign key)
    • ratingAmount
    • ratingContent
    • ratingDate
