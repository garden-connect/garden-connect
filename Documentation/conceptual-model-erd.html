ERD Diagram

Conceptual Model

    Profile

    • profileId (primary key)
    • profileActivationToken (for account verification)
    • profileEmail
    • profileHash (for account password)
    • profileName

    Posts

    • postId (primary key)
    • postProfileId (foreign key)
    • postCategory
    • postContent
    • postDate
    • postPicture

    Conversation

    • conversationId (primary key)
    • conversationPostID (foreign key)
    • conversationProfileId (foreign key)
    • conversationDate
    • conversationContent

    Rating/Review

    • ratingProfileId1 (foreign key) (of person leaving the review)
    • ratingProfileId2 (foreign key) (of person getting reviewed)
    • ratingAmount
    • ratingContent
    • ratingDate
