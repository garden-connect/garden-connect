ERD Diagram

Conceptual Model

    Profile

    • profileID (primary key)
    • profileActivationToken (for account verification)
    • profileEmail
    • profileHash (for account password)

    Posts

    • postID (primary key)
    • postCategory
    • postProfileID (foreign key)
    • postContent
    • postDate

    Conversation

    • conversationID (primary key)
    • conversationPostID (foreign key)
    • conversationDate
    • conversationProfileID (foreign key)
    • conversationContent

    Rating/Review

    • ratingID (primary key)
    • ratingAmount
    • ratingProfileID (foreign key) (of person leaving the review)
    • ratingContent

    Following

    • followingProfileID1 (foreign key)
    • followingProfileID2 (foreign key)
