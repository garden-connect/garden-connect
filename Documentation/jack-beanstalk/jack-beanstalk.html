Jack Beanstalk

Profession:

Novice Gardener, looking to expand his knowledge base as well as connect with people of similar interests in the area.

Technology:

Windows base user with an Acer Aspire C24 27-inch All-in-one monitor with home Wi-Fi. Jack also has a Samsung Galaxy S9 with a 4G data connection.

Attitudes and Behaviors:

Jack is fairly familiar with modern tech and willing to learn new things. He likes browsing social and e-commerce sites but doesn't like to spend too long doing so. Jack likes connecting with other users so that he can use their knowledge and offer some of his own.

Frustrations and Needs:

Jack doesn't like websites that rely on likes driving content, but he enjoys being able to revisit posts.

Goals:

Jack is looking to expand his bean garden as well as connect to other gardeners in the area who might have advice for first-timers.

User Story:

As a first-time user, I want to set up a profile and start connecting with people nearby.

Interaction Flow

Precondition: Jack is not yet a user, he then goes to the site to sign up.

Post-condition: Jack is a registered user and will be able to follow others and communicate on the app as well as having a bio and following some other users on the site.

  • Jack wants to sign up for our website to connect with other gardeners and navigates to the site and clicks on the "sign up" button.
  • The site gives him a form to tie his email to a unique username and set up account information.
  • Jack makes a username and password to access his account.
  • The site sends an email to the address to notify the owner of their email being used.
  • Jack then clicks on the button that opens up his profile page.
  • The site offers a form to fill out to share some basic details of the user.
  • Jack fills this out to the extent he wants and then clicks the "save changes" button.
  • The site saves the information and shows Jack his full profile page.
  • Jack then navigates to the main feed by clicking the "Feed" button.
  • The site takes him to a feed of current posts by other users.
  • He finds a post about someone looking to get rid of magic beans and clicks on the post.
  • The site has a button to message the user or review the post.
  • Jack clicks on the Review button.
  • The site opens a page to leave a review of the user as well as read other reviews.
  • Jack types a comment about how helpful the post was for his gardening and clicks on the "Post" button.
  • The site leaves the review for the poster to find and sends them a notification email.
  • Jack leaves for a bit to check on his new bean garden.
  • The site takes him to profile of the poster showing their recent posts.
